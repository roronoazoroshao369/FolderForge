# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in FolderForge a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, give actionable technical feedback, assume good intent while
addressing harmful impact, and keep discussions focused on the project.
Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, personal attacks, doxxing, and
publishing private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through the
contact method in the repository owner's GitHub profile. Maintainers may edit or
remove content and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when needed.
Reports will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible.

This policy is based on Contributor Covenant 2.1.
